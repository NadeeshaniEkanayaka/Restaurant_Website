About Us

Welcome to Signature Cuisine

Signature Cuisine is a premier dining experience that brings together the finest culinary delights and an inviting ambiance. With a team of talented chefs, dedicated staff, and a passion for exceptional service, we aim to create memorable moments for every guest.

Our History

Signature Cuisine started its journey in 1992, founded by Mr. Ekanayaka. With a vision to create a dining destination that celebrates gastronomy and hospitality, Mr. Ekanayaka embarked on a culinary adventure that has become a renowned establishment today. Since its inception, Signature Cuisine has been committed to delivering culinary excellence and outstanding service.
